Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

At Bhole Baba Investments, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide through our lead forms and customer relationship management system.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit your details through our lead forms on Facebook, WhatsApp, website, or other platforms, we collect and store the following information in our system:

  • Name: Your full name as provided
  • Email Address: For sending information and updates about our investment services
  • Phone Number: To contact you regarding your investment inquiries
  • Location/City: To understand your geographic location
  • Investment Preferences: Information about your investment interests and requirements
  • Source of Lead: Which platform or campaign you came from (Facebook, WhatsApp, referral, etc.)
  • Interaction History: Records of our communications with you, including calls, messages, and meetings
Important: We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us. You have complete control over what information you share.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information stored in our system is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Contact and Communication: To reach out to you via phone, email, or WhatsApp regarding investment opportunities and services
  • Service Delivery: To provide you with information about investment plans, property details, and financial products
  • Follow-up: To maintain communication records and follow up on your investment inquiries
  • Customer Relationship Management: To track your preferences, requirements, and interaction history for better service
  • Personalized Recommendations: To suggest investment options that match your stated interests and requirements
  • Appointment Scheduling: To coordinate meetings, site visits, and consultations

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your information:

  • Secure System: Your data is stored in a password-protected database with restricted access
  • Limited Access: Only authorized team members who need to contact you have access to your information
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes
  • Data Encryption: Your information is protected using industry-standard security practices
We Never Share Your Data: Your contact details and personal information remain confidential and are used solely by Bhole Baba Investments to serve you better.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information in our system for the following periods:

  • Active leads and customers: Maintained for the duration of our business relationship
  • Inactive leads: Retained for up to 2-3 years for potential future opportunities
  • Upon your request: We will promptly delete your information from our system

5. Your Rights

You have complete control over your personal information. You can:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of the information we have stored about you
  • Update Your Details: Inform us of any changes to your contact information or preferences
  • Delete Your Data: Request complete removal of your information from our system
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from our communications at any time
  • Restrict Communication: Choose your preferred method and frequency of contact

6. Marketing Communications

When you provide your details, you consent to receive communications from Bhole Baba Investments about:

  • Investment opportunities and property listings
  • Financial products and services
  • Company updates and news
  • Special offers and promotions

You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us or clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.
