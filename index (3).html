We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement comprehensive measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

4.1 Security Measures Include:

  • Encryption: Industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage
  • Access Controls: Strict limitations on who can access your information within our organization

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Specifically:
